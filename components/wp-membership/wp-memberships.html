ADD-ONS

By empowering our community to take on active roles, we can create a stronger food ecosystem.

TRIBE

YFood Tribe members are Food Tech startups and scaleups that have been reviewed and accredited using the YFood methodology.

GAME-CHANGER

YFood Game-Changers work with YFood to educate, motivate and empower the community to create a better food ecosystem. They do this by creating informative and engaging content and events in line with YFood guidelines and tried and tested formats. They hold important roles and highly recognised profiles in the community. The community can search by their Game-changer profile to discover the content created by them.

See More

DISRUPTORS

YFood Disruptors invest time and energy in the YFood community by educating the community on how their product can make a difference to the food ecosystem. They do this by showcasing their product at relevant themed events and creating educational demo and pitch videos.

See More

TRAILBLAZER

YFood Trailblazers are actively seeking out connections, partnerships and relationships to grow their business. They are true innovators with an entrepreneurial mindset and have been accredited by YFood, therefore have our stamp of approval. They are looking to gain feedback and create pilots and partnerships with Pioneers and Growth Partners in order to improve their product and technology, take it to the next level and make it accessible.

See More

LINK-UP

YFood Link-Up members are actively seeking out connections to grow their business. They are looking to gain feedback and create pilots and partnerships with Pioneers and Growth Partners in order to improve their product and technology, take it to the next level and make it accessible.

YFood Connectors are there to support the community with their innovation and growth goals. They connect with the community in a meaningful way, as well as ensure the community can actively connect to the relevant people within their organisations and networks.

See More

CREATOR

YFood Creators work with YFood to create informative and engaging content in line with YFood guidelines and tried and tested formats. They hold important roles and highly recognised profiles in the community. The community can search by their Creator profile to discover the content created by them.

See More

PIONEER

YFood Pioneers are actively looking to put partnerships and pilots in place with the YFood community. They can connect, create and more. They also benefit from YFoods curated Discover & Connect service annually, to support them with their efforts seeking out the best new and innovative solutions.

*You have to qualify for this plan

See More

GROWTH

The YFood Growth Partners are leading partners of our Growth Progammes, they can connect, create, trailblaze and more. They work directly with YFood to support and grow the talented startups and scaleups that have been selected. The Growth Programmes support selected startups to achieve their full potential through direct YFood involvement and a curated support accessing the YFood community, experts, partners and investors.

*You have to qualify for this plan
